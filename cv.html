Executive Summary

"In the theater of law, technical proficiency is the entry fee — but strategic foresight is the winning edge. I don't process files; I architect solutions."

An LLB graduate and aspiring Candidate Attorney defined by a high-performance mindset. My legal training at the University of the Free State forged a disciplined analytical engine capable of navigating complex mercantile and corporate environments. Having completed a 400-hour intensive PVT programme, I have transitioned from theory to substantive execution — specialising in identifying legal friction through precision-drafted Notices of Exception, Special Pleas, and procedural strategy.
